== ALLERGEN NOTICE ==

Our kitchen handles gluten, dairy, eggs, nuts, shellfish, and fish.

For a full allergen breakdown per item, ask your server for the allergen menu.
Gluten-free pasta and pizza bases are available on request for an additional charge of 2 USD.
