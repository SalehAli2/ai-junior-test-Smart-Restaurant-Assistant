NOVABITE RESTAURANTS - LOYALTY PROGRAM

PROGRAM NAME
NovaBite Rewards

EARNING POINTS
- Customers earn 1 point for every 1 USD spent.
- Points are credited to the account within 24 hours of a completed purchase.
- Points cannot be earned on discounted items, promotional offers, or gift card purchases.
- Points are only earned on dine-in and takeaway orders. Delivery orders through third-party apps are excluded.
- Double points are awarded every Wednesday for dine-in orders.

REDEEMING POINTS
- 100 points = 5 USD discount on your bill.
- Minimum redemption is 100 points per transaction.
- Points cannot be redeemed as cash.
- Points cannot be combined with other discounts or promotional vouchers.
- A maximum of 500 points can be redeemed per single transaction.

ACCOUNT & MEMBERSHIP
- Membership is free. Sign up via the NovaBite app or at any branch.
- Each customer is allowed one loyalty account only.
- Accounts are personal and non-transferable.
- Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.
- NovaBite reserves the right to suspend accounts with suspicious activity.

BIRTHDAY REWARD
- Members receive a free dessert during their birthday month.
- The birthday reward is valid for dine-in only.
- Valid ID may be required to claim the birthday reward.
- The birthday reward cannot be exchanged for cash or other items.

TIERS
Keywords: membership tiers, silver gold platinum, loyalty levels, tier status, rewards tiers

- Silver: 0 – 499 points earned lifetime
- Gold: 500 – 1499 points earned lifetime
- Platinum: 1500+ points earned lifetime
- Gold members receive priority reservations.
- Platinum members receive a complimentary starter on every visit.
- Tier status is reviewed every 12 months.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT
- For points disputes contact support@novabite.com within 7 days of the transaction.
- Allow up to 5 business days for points disputes to be resolved.
